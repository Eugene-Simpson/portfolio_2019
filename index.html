About Me

Hello,
Namaskara!

I am Eugene Simpson,
an Art Director, UI designer and Researcher based of Bangalore, India.

I am a passionate Art director in his late 20's, With 6+ years of hand-on multidisciplinary Digital Creative Experience specializing in Visual Design, Art Direction, rich User Experiences and Strategies. I have an eye for detail and a passion for what I do. Currently working with awesome guys at Moonraft Innovation Labs.

I basically believe in Minimalistic, clean approach and it is reflected in my Designs as well as my vector illustrations. I craft responsive experiences on desktop and mobile devices with an eye for exquisite design and lots of imagination, drawing inspiration from Music, Travel and Art.

Im also a proud founder of Chriscrafts Studios, a dedicated online platform where i share soulful and beautiful classic christian music.
